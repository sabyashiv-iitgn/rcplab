# Open Positions

We are looking for people at all levels who are interested in cognitive science, computational
neuroscience, behavioral experiments, Bayesian modeling, reinforcement learning, motor control, and
perception.

- **Postdocs:** we currently have two open postdoctoral positions. See the
  <a href="https://iitgn.ac.in/careers/staff">IIT Gandhinagar staff careers page</a>
  for details and application instructions.
- **Ph.D. students:** see the
  <a href="https://cogs.iitgn.ac.in/newcogs/">Department of Cognitive and Brain Sciences homepage</a>
  for admissions information.
- **Master's students:** see the
  <a href="https://cogs.iitgn.ac.in/newcogs/">Department of Cognitive and Brain Sciences homepage</a>
  for admissions information and final-year thesis opportunities.
- **Research assistants:** we do not currently have funded research-assistant positions. However,
  people who would like to volunteer as an RA and gain experience working in a research lab are
  highly encouraged to complete the
  [volunteer RA interest form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScmBO4t1jHQbeE_W2i9oBzHDVK6uD3JKJS-RmVEM5K5CUoYhQ/viewform?usp=publish-editor).
