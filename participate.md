# Participate in our research

RCP Lab recruits participants for experiments on a need basis. The forms below add you to a
recruitment list so that we can contact you when a potentially suitable study is available; joining a
list does not guarantee selection for a study.

Participants must be **18–65 years old**. Individual experiments may have further eligibility
criteria—including visual acuity, colour vision, health history, prior experience, or device
requirements—that determine final participation.
